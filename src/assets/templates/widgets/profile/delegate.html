Delegates are individuals who can act on a student's behalf by paying bills, viewing academic information, etc. They are often parents or guardians of students. Students choose which privileges to delegate, and can change those privileges at any time.

Create or manage delegates

Other information:

  • Free/busy: To give a delegate a free/busy view of your bCal calendar, follow the bCal instructions.
  • Profile and bConnected content: Delegates can view neither profile information nor bConnected content: mail, calendar, etc.
  • Advisors: Advisors can view all financial and academic information shown in CalCentral but cannot see Profile or bConnected content.
